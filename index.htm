About Us

Stacy Bengtson has over 20 years of experience designing commercial workplace interiors, primarily in Portland, OR, with a formative stint in NYC. With a strong foundation in building code and a background in Design & Fine Art, Stacy combines technical expertise with a creative mindset to deliver thoughtful and functional workspaces. She excels at building long-term client relationships through her industrious work ethic and fresh perspective, approaching every project with a beginner’s mindset and an unwavering commitment to creating quality work.
